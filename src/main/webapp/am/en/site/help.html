              How to enable Browser Features

              • How to enable Java Scripts
                If Java Script is not enabled on your browser, the site may not display as designed. Please enable your browser for Java Scripts by changing the security settings of your browser.

              •  

              • How to enable Cookies 
                If Cookies is disabled on your browser, the site may not display as designed. Please enable your browser for cookies by changing the Privacy settings of your browser and accept cookies to access the full functionality of Asia Miles website.

              •  

              • How to enable SSL 
                If SSL is disabled on your browser, the site may not display as designed. Please enable your browser for SSL by changing the Security settings of your browser.

              •  

              • How to enable Pop-ups 
                If the pop-ups are disabled/blocked on your browser, the site may not display as designed. Please enable your browser for pop-ups by changing the settings of your browser, security software or toolbars.

              •  

              • How to enable Flash 
                If the Flash is not installed/enabled, the Homepage and Your Account Overview page may not display as designed. Please download the Adobe Flash Player.
