              Our Company

              Asia Miles, Asia’s leading travel and lifestyle rewards programme, was launched in February 1999 and now has over 8 million members and over 700 programme partners worldwide. Asia Miles offers members extensive opportunities to earn miles by spending daily from a wide range of travel and lifestyle categories including flights, hotels, dining, financial services, retail, technology brands, and many others. With 24 airline partners, Asia Miles members can earn miles when flying to more than 1,000 destinations worldwide. With nearly 400 dining partners to choose from, Asia Miles offers one of the largest ranges of lifestyle food & beverage options in Asia.

              In addition to flight awards, Asia Miles members can also redeem miles and enjoy over 1,800 fabulous travel and lifestyle awards such as hotel stays, dining package, shopping vouchers, electronics, concert tickets, and other leisure items.

              Asia Miles is open to anyone aged 2 or above and free to join.

              Asia Miles is managed and operated by Asia Miles Limited, a wholly owned subsidiary of Cathay Pacific Airways Limited.

              Our Vision

              We aim to be Asia’s lifestyle rewards programme of choice, adding value to members, partners and employees alike.

              Our Values

              Our core values govern every level of our business, from basic customer service right up our large-scale future plans.
