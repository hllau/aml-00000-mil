              Asia Miles Service Desk, Hong Kong International Airport

              There are two service desks in the Departures Hall, Terminal 1 of Hong Kong International Airport:

              • Aisle B: open daily from 7:00am to 11:45pm
              • Aisle H: open daily from 6:00am to 9:30pm


              Member services include:

              • Programme information
              • Mileage checking
              • Missing mileage credits - for Cathay Pacific and Dragonair flights
              • Itinerary changes
              • Administration e.g. personal information changes
              • Redemption bookings
              • Redemption ticket issuance and pick up (Aisle B Service desk only)
              • Instant redemption (upgrades, extra baggage and Extra legroom Seats) on Cathay Pacific and Dragonair flights
              • Asia Miles enrolment
