              Flight Award Finder

              You can use the Flight Award Finder to calculate the number of miles required to redeem any Asia Miles or oneworld award.

              For Cathay Pacific, only fare classes Y, B, H, K and M are eligible for Economy to Premium Economy Class Upgrade Awards. Only fare classes W and R are eligible for Premium Economy to Business Class Upgrade Awards. Only fare classes J, C, D and I are eligible for Business to First Class Upgrade Awards.
              For Dragonair, only fare classes Y, B, H, K and M are eligible for Economy to Business Class Upgrade Awards. Only fare classes J, C, D and I are eligible for Business to First Class Upgrade Awards.
              Upgrade Awards are not applicable on codeshare flights.
              Mixed class and mixed carrier awards do not apply to class upgrades and companion tickets.
              For more details of all airline partners, please refer to the Airline Partners Awards Chart.

              Upgrade Awards are only applicable from Economy Class to Business Class for flights without Premium Economy Class.
